# 44-FloTo

Florian Tomasin. Building for the next generation of operators, founders, and ideas.

CCO by day at a founder-led ERP company in the DACH food industry. Builder by every other measure. I work where org design, software, and customer reality collide — and I'm increasingly interested in what comes after the way Mittelstand has done things for the last thirty years.

## Now

- **44up** — a movement, not a project. Growth beyond the 44th floor. More as pieces ship.
- **Org patterns for the next generation of Mittelstand.** What scales, what breaks, what no one writes down because it's too obvious to insiders.
- **ERP and ticketing for humans, not IT departments.** Software the next generation of business owners will actually choose to use.
- **The operator-builder hybrid.** Running a function and shipping product at the same time, without lying to either side.

## This profile

A workshop. Patterns I keep returning to, tools I've sharpened in real situations, drafts in progress. Some polished, some deliberately not. Pinned repos point to whatever's most current.
